About the Event

Event Name: Khula Maidaan
Event Head: Prerna Agarwal (8001104555), Kasturi Mohit (7063279358)
Description: Khula Maidaan is an event based on technical and creative skills. The participants will have to design products based on the topics given and come up with plans to commercialize their products in the real world market. They may or may not use technical tools in doing so.
No. of Rounds: 2
No. of members in a team: 3
Judging criteria:
Round 1: Teams will be given fun ideas to base their products on. They have to draw a draft of their ideas in pen and paper. Marking will depend on the creativity of the participants.
Round 2: Teams will be given topics and a round of questions, they will have to create a product identity along with logo, tagline, etc. The participants will also have to develop a market strategy to make the product competitive. They have to compile their work in the form of a presentation.
Marking will be divided into three parts:

  • 1) Creativity of the participants (20).
  • 2) Marketing strategies used (20).
  • 3) Technical tools used and skills (10)
