About the Event

Electronic circuits might have taken a long time to be invented but it did not take a long time to make its presence in all walks of life. From mobile phone to satellites, everything depends on electronic circuits. Aarohan 2017 provides a platform for all talented and electronics zealot to showcase their talent in the field of circuit designing and debugging. In this three stage event,participants will have to race against time as they compete with one another to make the best possible circuit and prove their control over circuits.
Rules and Regulation:

  • 1. Participants can participate in a team of 3.
  • 2. Decision of evaluator will be final.

No. of Rounds: 3
Round 1: This round will check your knowledge on electronics. It's an objective type quiz on Basic Electronics and aptitude. These questions have to be solved within 30 minutes. Selected teams will move to next round.
Round 2: This will check your hand on electronic devices. It's a circuit debugging competition. Win the Race against other teams and join us in our next round.
Round 3: Prove your dominance over ICs. Enhance your engineering skill by applying your practical knowledge. Design a circuit before other teams do.
No. of members in a team: 3
Judging criteria: Fastest hands first. In case of tie judgement is based on approach.
