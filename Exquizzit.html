About the Event

You can't simply be doing "nothing". Everything you do does count in some queer insane way or the other. Everything: be it slumping on to the couch, be it lousily perching over the 42" led screen, be it bumping wrappers at a stadium, be it drooling over the hot guy in the silver screen or be it fantasizing the kick of tomorrowland, it does count. Speaking the least of it, in "EXQUIZZIT" this 'everything', seasoned with a bit of retentiveness, does wonders. Don't be surprised to see your interests and drawing room entertainment get you aces in this round of "EXQUIZZIT"
