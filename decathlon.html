About the Event

Decathlon is a online coding event hosted on www.codechef.com in association with RECursion, NITD Programming group.

  1. You need to have a codechef account. If not, then register at www.codechef.com. Its free!!
  2. Event is on 10th of February,2017

  4. From 9:30 PM to 12:00 AM IST
  5. Its an Individual event. The one who solves most problems will be the winner.
