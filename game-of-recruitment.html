About the Event

This particular event is designed keeping in mind the thrill, excitement and anxiety of the entire placement process in colleges. This event lets the participating audience get a feel of the recruitment process and also learn something apart from the fun part. Thus, the idea behind this event is to amalgamate fun along with something useful.
