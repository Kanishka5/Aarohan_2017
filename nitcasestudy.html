About the Event

Wherever you see a successful business, someone once made a courageous decision. - Peter Drucker

This Aarohan brings to the ultimate test of your analytical and decision making skills through the NIT Case Study Challenge. Every CEO faces tough situations in which he must analyze the scenario and take the best decision in favor of the company. The NIT Case Study Challenge will put every participant in the shoes of a CEO and test their managerial skills. So we invite all aspiring business leaders to prove their wit in the NIT Case Study Challenge.
