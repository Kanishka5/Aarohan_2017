About the Event

Event Name : JOURNO DETECTIVE
Event Head : Soham Chaudhuri
Description : Delve into a world of suspense and mystery. Rack your brains, scamper and scurry. This Aarohan pump up for the thrill. Battle it out to unravel the enigma!. Play as the detective and race against time to find the culprit.
Prelims consist of a pen-paper round . Top 10 teams reach the finals. The teams in the final round would have to solve a mystery based on clues collected from various checkpoints, the key to finding a checkpoint will be given as an encryption at the proceeding checkpoint, with the arena being the starting point . The team that solves the mystery at the earliest would be declared as the winner.
No. Of Rounds : TWO
No. Of Members in a team : FOUR
Judging Criteria : In the prelims , on the basis of the number of questions answered correctly by the teams , selection will be done and only 10 teams will reach the finals . In the final round , the first team which reaches the final checkpoint and successfully solves the mystery will be declared as the winner.
