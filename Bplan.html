About the Event

"It's not about ideas. It's about making ideas happen." - Scott Belsky

Ever had an entrepreneurial idea, for which you had the utmost conviction, to scale it beyond imagination and to emerge as a game changer? Look no further, we strive to provide you the requisite impetus to drive your seminal ideas and achieve immensurate success.

If you think you've got the calibre and the ambition to rise to those meteoric heights then this is the right forum to show your excellence.
