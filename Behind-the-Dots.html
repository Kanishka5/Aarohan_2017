About the Event

"Marketing is no longer about the stuff that you have, but the stories that you tell." Using charm and logic, talk your way to pitch for the given product. Get ready and be a part of the ultimate pitching experience. Unveil your whacky ideas and quirky business fundas as you race past others in wit, intelligence and marketing strategy. Make sure your business quotient counts, as you elevate yourself to a whole new level never seen before. The dots are summoning those with zeal to achieve and desire to conquer. Aarohan - The Annual Techno Management Fest presents Behind the Dots. We heartily welcome you to this maze of thrill and excitement!

1. There will be three rounds in 'Behind the Dots. You have to make a team of four members to be able to participate in this event. All the teams that register for the event will get to sit in the first round.

2. First Round: The first round will comprise of audio-visual questions testing your general acumen. Based on the final scores at the end of the first round, top six teams will qualify for the second round.

3. Second Round:

  • In the second round, the teams will have to look for 'dots' which will be hidden all over a place inside the campus (this place will be revealed to the participants on-the-spot).
  • These dots will have 4X4 grid-mazes containing questions to be answered in a stipulated time, finding the best path possible.
  • At the end of the maze there will be a riddle pointing to a particular place in the campus.
  • If the teams are able to solve their respective riddles, they rush to that place, find clues to guess three commercial products.
  • The score at the end of this round will decide which four teams out of the six qualify for the final round.

    • 4. Final Round: On successfully deciphering the clues correctly, the teams will get to choose one of the products to make a spoof about. The team that manages to entertain the audience the most and grabs the maximum marks from the jury and is able to score the cumulative maximum points, bags the title of the winning team!
