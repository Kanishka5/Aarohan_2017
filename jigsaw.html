About the Event

Description: The Game is basically about solving a puzzle by placing the boxes in the rack to crack the password for opening the Exit Door. The Bots have to reach the rack at some height by lift and have to move on the rails at the same height.

No. of Rounds: 2

No. of members in a team: 5 (MAXIMUM)

    Judging criteria:
  • 1.Time Taken to Complete the Event.
  • 2.Points awarded for completing the tasks and filling each box correctly in rack.

Event Head: SAGAR RAJU BAVASKAR
