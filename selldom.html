About the Event

A sale is not something you pursue; it’s what happens to you when you are immersed in serving your customer. In sales, it’s not what you say; it’s how they perceive what you say.

This Aarohan, come over the obstacles of need, money, hurry, desire and trust to emerge as the best when it comes to selling your product. Because in the words of R. L. Stevenson, “Everyone lives by selling something.”
No. of Rounds: 2
No. of members in a team: 4
Judging criteria: The top 2 teams which earn the maximum profit are adjudged 1st and 2nd.
